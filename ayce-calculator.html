About the prices. KBBQ and hotpot per-item retail prices are estimated against typical US AYCE-market à la carte rates because the source menus only show flat AYCE pricing. Sushi prices are taken directly from a real Southern California AYCE Sushi à la carte menu. Wholesale figures are estimated using 30–45% food-cost ratios; actual procurement varies by supplier, grade, and contract volume. Calorie counts are USDA-aligned ballparks for cooked weight.

About the cities. Ten most populous US metros with meaningful AYCE concentration. Phoenix and Miami excluded for thin AYCE presence; Bay Area and Seattle included as hubs. Regional multipliers adjust retail only.
